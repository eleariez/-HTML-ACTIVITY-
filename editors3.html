Exploring Social Media and Teen Life

Home Editor's Note Articles Updates

Note ᝰ.ᐟ

Social media plays a major role in the daily lives of teenagers. It shapes how they communicate, learn, and express themselves. While it provides many opportunities for connection and creativity, it also raises concerns about mental health, online safety, and excessive screen time.

This digital magazine explores the influence of social media on teenagers, examining both the positive and negative impacts. Through research-based articles and discussions, we aim to raise awareness and encourage responsible use of technology among students.

Insight 🔎

As students growing up in a digital world, we often rely on social media to connect with friends and stay informed. However, it is important to remember that balance is key. By understanding both the advantages and the risks, teenagers can develop healthier digital habits and become responsible digital citizens.
