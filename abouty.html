About Gossip Girl

Gossip Girl is a popular American teen drama television series based on the book series by Cecily von Ziegesar. The show revolves around the lives of privileged teenagers living in Manhattan's Upper East Side, exploring themes of friendship, rivalry, romance, and secrets, all narrated by the mysterious "Gossip Girl".



Serena van der Woodsen
The beautiful and charismatic It Girl of the Upper East Side, known for her charm, complicated relationships, and wild past.
Blair Waldorf
The Queen Bee of the Upper East Side with a sharp wit, strong personality, and fierce loyalty to her friends and family.
