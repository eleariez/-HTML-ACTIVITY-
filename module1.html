Introduction to Hyperlinks

Hyperlinks are one of the most important parts of the web. They allow users to move from one webpage to another. Without hyperlinks, websites would not be connected.

The Anchor Tag

The HTML tag used to create links is the <a> tag.

<a href="https://www.google.com">Visit Google</a>
